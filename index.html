Customer Care

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I wash and care for my Revora underwear?

To maximize the life of your micro-modal garments, we recommend hand washing in cold water or machine washing on a delicate cycle in a mesh laundry bag. Avoid bleaching, lay flat to dry in shade, and do not iron or dry clean.

What is your exchange and resizing policy?

We want you to feel completely comfortable. If the fit isn't absolutely perfect, we offer a complimentary size exchange on your first order within 30 days of purchase. Simply print out our prepaid label and send it back.

Are the materials sustainable?

Yes. Our modal fibers are sourced from FSC-certified sustainable Austrian beechwood forests. The fiber manufacturing process uses a closed-loop system recycling 95% of water and solvents, creating a carbon-neutral raw material.
